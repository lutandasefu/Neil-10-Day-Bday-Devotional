Love Her Holy

Neil's 10-Day Devotional Journal

You have loved with everything you have, and the world has not always known what to do with that.

But God does. He made you this way on purpose.

This journal is for you. Alongside the word of God and conversations with people you trust, I hope it helps you establish your position as a single man about to venture into a fruitful relationship. I am no expert. But in combination with what I know, you will be wiser when it comes to this chapter in your life.

These ten days are not about becoming less. They are about becoming more rightly ordered. About loving the way God loves. About becoming the man worthy of the woman you are praying for. About loving her holy, before you even meet her... or maybe you already have.

With love, Lulu Pops (This Nickname will stick by force!)
