Neil's 10-Day Devotional Journal

This journal is for you — the one whose heart is too big for the world's small definitions of love. You have loved with everything you have, and the world has not always known what to do with that.

These ten days are not about becoming less — they are about becoming more rightly ordered. About learning to love the way God loves. About becoming the man worthy of the woman you are praying for. About loving her holy, before you even meet her.

With love, your sister in faith — and your biggest fan. 🌹
